Overview of TechFinder

In 2010, TechSoup Global and NTEN decided to retire their site TechFinder.org, passing the data on to Aspiration with an invitation to create a next-generation version. For much of the past decade, TechFinder served as a resource to help nonprofits identify technology service providers, and Aspiration is proud to be heading up efforts to get TechFinder back online.

This summer, Aspiration's intern Caroline began researching other search engines, writing down ideas, sketching out pages, and collecting feedback from the community in order to move forward with this project. Check out Project Resources for some examples, and "How I Spent My Summer for further details. Feel free to contact us anytime at tfprototype@aspirationtech.org!
